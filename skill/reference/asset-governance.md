# Asset: Governance

How the project is run: who decides what, and how someone earns a seat. Convention: the
CNCF/Apache-style GOVERNANCE.md — roles, decision process, and the maintainer ladder.
Structure check: `mari asset check <file>`. Scaffold: `mari asset scaffold governance "<project>"`.

## Canonical structure (required)
- **Roles** — contributors, maintainers, leads; the concrete rights and duties of each.
- **Decision Making** — how decisions are made (lazy consensus, voting thresholds).
- **Maintainers** — the measurable path to becoming one, and how one steps down or is removed.

Recommended: **Meetings**, **Code of Conduct**, **Changing This Document** (amendment process).

## Tone
- Define roles by what they can *do* and are *responsible for*, not by title alone.
- Make the contributor→maintainer path explicit and measurable — no "at the maintainers' discretion".
- State how the governance doc itself is amended; governance that can't change is fiction.

## Review rubric
1. All required sections present.
2. Each role lists concrete rights/responsibilities.
3. The decision process names the mechanism and threshold, not just "consensus".
4. The maintainer ladder is measurable (what earns it, what loses it).
5. An amendment process exists.

Leans on: structure check + `passive-voice`, `nominalization`, `vague-attribution`.
