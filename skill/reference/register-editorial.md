# Register: editorial (blog posts, essays, long-form)

A human voice with a point of view. The register where personality is the product.

## Bar
- **Voice:** first or second person welcome; opinion is allowed and expected.
- **Rhythm:** vary sentence length deliberately — short for emphasis, long for flow. Monotone
  cadence is the tell.
- **Openings & closings:** earn them. No "In today's fast-paced world"; no "In conclusion" that
  restates the intro. Open on a concrete hook; end on a specific note.
- **Specifics:** anecdote, example, and detail carry editorial writing. Generic = dead.

## Detector emphasis
Strict on: `cliche-opener`, `conclusion-restate`, `manufactured-contrast`, `vague-attribution`,
cadence/`tricolon-overuse`. Relaxed on: em-dash, contractions, sentence-length ceiling (longer
sentences are fine when the rhythm earns them).
