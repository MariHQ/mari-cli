# Register: marketing (landing copy, announcements, campaigns)

Persuade a skeptical reader with specifics, not adjectives.

## Bar
- **Voice:** confident, concrete, benefit-led. Show the value; don't assert it.
- **Sentence ceiling:** ~30 words, with deliberate rhythm variation (short punchy lines are fine).
- **Specificity over superlatives:** replace "world-class / best-in-class / cutting-edge" with a
  verifiable claim. A number beats an adjective.
- **Restraint:** one strong claim lands; five stacked superlatives read as hype and AI slop.

## Detector emphasis
Strict on: `marketing-buzzword`, `manufactured-contrast`, `significance-boilerplate`,
`emoji-decoration`, `excessive-bold`, hype. Relaxed on: em-dash and emphasis (a little flair is
on-brand), sentence length.
