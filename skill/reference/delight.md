# delight — add memorable, human touches

The restraint-first enhancement. A sharp opening, a concrete example, one well-placed specific
detail — without tipping into cute or slop.

## Flow
1. Read the piece and find the flat spots: a generic opener, an abstract claim that wants a
   concrete example, a forgettable close.
2. Add one or two human touches where they earn their place: a vivid specific over a
   generality, a real example over a hypothetical, a sharper first line.
3. Run the detector — make sure your "delight" didn't reintroduce slop (clichés, manufactured
   contrast, exclamation). Delight that reads as AI flourish is worse than plain.

## Guardrails
- Restraint first. One memorable detail beats five cute ones.
- If a touch doesn't serve the reader's understanding or pleasure, cut it.

Leans on: Family A (so the additions don't read as slop) + judgment.
